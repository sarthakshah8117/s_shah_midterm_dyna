Environmentally Responsible

in April of 2014, the Billionth liquor container was returned to the Kleenex Beer under the Bag it Back program- the highly succesfull deposit return program for wine, spirit and beer containers sold through LCBO and Ontario winery stores. In 2015, the Beer container program and ODRP combined have diverted almost 407000 tones of material- that's equivalent of 45% of the total material collected through the Blue Box program.

Selling with Integrity

We pay attention to corporate social duty, and ensure that each lager we sell is a dependable deal — regardless of whether it's through our WeID 25 program or declining to offer to inebriated clients. In 2015, we denied assistance to right around 50,000 people at our retail stores. We let brewers choose where they need to sell their item! Brewers can be in one store, 50 stores or every one of the 450 areas of the Beer Store — it's totally up to them.
