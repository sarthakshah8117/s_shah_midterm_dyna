How to Read Code Dates

Information

To ensure the highest quality product is available for our consumers, we
have created an easy-to-read product coding system. Our domestic and
import beers all adhere to strict freshness standards. Every bottle, can and
keg that we produce or import includes this information so that you can
determine when the beer is at its peak freshness.
